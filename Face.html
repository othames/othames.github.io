Face

September 2024 - January 2025

‘Face’ is a book designed to educate and demonstrate the full range of available opportunities and expected outcomes when considering getting a facial piercing.

In order to explore the chronology of getting a face piercing, ‘Face’ begins with an overview of constraints that may inhibit getting a piercing, such as health conditions and lifestyle considerations. The book then steps into the piercing studio, showing common instruments, mechanical equipment, and the various types of jewelry to pick from. Next is the book's main emphasis, forty-four different variations of facial piercings, their piercing procedures, and the risks of each piercing. Finally, the book ends with instructions for healing and aftercare and an extensive gallery.

All photography, typesetting, and instrument design were done personally.
