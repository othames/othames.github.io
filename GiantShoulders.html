Giant Shoulders

February - April 2024

Several projects I made while interning for Giant Shoulders, a brand strategy and design agency. While employed, I divided my time between creating content for social media, supporting the branding process with design iteration and brand guideline creation, as well as researching and crafting brand strategy.

The pictured files include a website mockup I made for VINCIVR, a VR company transitioning from job training in offshore wind to secondary education. With this in mind, I wanted to cater to an administrative audience by including pastel colors and Sora, a soft transitional typeface with negative space that felt distinctly technological.

Additionally, I have included several mock-ups for a Kaplan Consulting flier.
