Avenue Magazine Liminality

February - April 2024

Design work for The Avenue, Northeastern University's culture and fashion magazine. This shoot was centered around liminal spaces, and so emulating this environment through each of my design choices became my objective. This appears most in my photography treatment as I wanted every page to contain textures of liminality, so I chose to overlay strong images onto the concrete and plaster that framed our shoot. With this in mind, the negative space surrounding the text feels ripped out of this environment and a small respite from the grit and grime.
