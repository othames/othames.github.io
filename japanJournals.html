Journal Spread Designs

May - June 2023

These spreads are part of a 35-day journaling project I undertook while abroad in Japan. Using photos I took on the given days as well as colors and shapes formed in InDesign, I created various themes representative of the moods that came through in
my writing.

My desire for this project was to gain an aptitude for creating design spreads, and in order to do that I attempted to make each spread unique and separate from elements I had already used.
