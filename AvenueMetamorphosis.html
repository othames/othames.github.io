Avenue Magazine Metamorphosis

February - April 2024

Photography and Design work for The Avenue, Northeastern Univeristy’s culture and fashion magazine. The theme of this edition was ‘Play’ which I interpreted through a graphic design lens as the act of playing with a project or, more simply, iterating. As the photographer, I wanted to create a storyline representative of the iterations a person goes through to get to a stage of contentedness or playfulness, and the three stages of metamorphosis seemed to epitomize this movement. Pupa would express feelings of loss, pain, and disorientation, Cocoon would be a marker of the development of understanding but still with room for more, while Moth would represent joy. freedom. feeling alive, and being able to be at play in your body per se.
