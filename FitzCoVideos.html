'FITZ & CO. Video Works

January - May 2025

Several video projects I made while under the employment of FITZ & Co., a social media management and consulting agency.

Oftentimes these were highlight reels for important events involving the FITZ Team. Members of the team, who were at these events, would capture videos or images and it was my task to sort through and assemble a reel highlighting particular artists, works, or guests. Notable events include India Art Fair, Hong Kong Art Week, and Blackbook: Mother Nature in the Bardo.
