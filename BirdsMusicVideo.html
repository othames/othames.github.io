'Birds' Music Video

June - July 2025

A promotional music video for Jesse Leor’s song, Birds.

Birds is a love song focussing on the remembrance of old love and the moments that defined it. For this reason, I chose to film the footage on a grainy children’s camera which I feel gives a nostalgic quality that looks a little different every time it is rewatched, similar to memories.

For social media viewing, the video was scaled to 1350 x 1080. This forced me to decide between cropping my shots or adding a border, however, I decided to alter between the two to emphasize the chorus. An embellishment was additionally added to soften the contrast between the footage and the border.
