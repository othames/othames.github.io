Ralph Appelbaum Exhibition Poster

January - February 2023

This poster covers the history of Ralph Appelbaum who is renowned for his accomplishment of transforming the field of Exhibition Design.

The poster was designed to question what an exhibition feels like in a two-dimensional setting, and by incorporating perspective alongside static photos and text, I created a sensation of depth within the piece.
