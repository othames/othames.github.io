Urnes Stave Church Brand Redesign

January - April 2023

This redesign of the Urnes Stave Church grew from an analysis of the physical characteristics, history, and cultural significance of the UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Known for its wooden appearance and its natural surroundings, the material identity of this structure played a major role in its design. The colors and logo are both evidence of this, however, the wooden cover of the information booklet which was created by laser-engraving the artwork and laser-cutting living hinges for bendability, is a staple of this identity.

Further components are the three-part New York Times advertisement, the stationery set, the website, and the informative site-sign translated into the six official UNESCO languages.
