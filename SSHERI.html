SSEHRI PFAS Project Lab Logo Redesign

December 2021

Social Science Environmental Health Research Institute is a Northeastern University Organization focused on forwarding social science-environmental health science collaborations.

I was asked to redesign the logo for
their PFAS Project Lab, which specializes in ‘studying social, scientific, and
political factors of per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances.’

I was involved in several iterations
with the client and landed on one
that currently remains on the organization's website.
