Ebadov Font

January - April 2022

Ebadov is a transitional serif typeface with select modern influences particularly in the serif. Immensely inspired by
William Martin’s Bulmer and
Giambattista Bodoni’s Bodoni typeface, Ebadov seeks to blend the boundaries between the known modern and transitional typefaces.

The typeface’s most striking features exist in its flattened bowl, ball terminals, and sharp contrast particularly seen in the ‘g’ and ‘7’ below.

The ‘Ebadov Sterio’ artwork was created by designing the visuals in Photoshop and then importing the text in Illustrator. I was inspired by looking through old Rolling Stones Magazines and drooling over the beautiful advertisements
they featured.
