Northeastern Art & Design Course Promotion Flyers

February 2022 - Present

Working through Northeastern’s Office of Art & Design, I was tasked with designing promotional course flyers with a number of design guidelines including an attached photo, a white background, and a 3x5 grid.

With the remaining design decisions up to me, I pushed myself to experiment and create dynamic compositions that played with the hierarchical balance of image and text.
