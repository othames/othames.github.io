Nature Track Reels

August 2025 - Present

I was asked to create a quick and punchy promotional reel for Nature Track, an organization focused on enabling wheelchair users to experience nature, and their partner Freedom Trax, a wheelchair attachment allowing for all-terrain mobility.

Nature Track provided me with a wide array of footage, often taken by volunteers on their day-to-day excursions. Quality and repetitiveness ended up being my biggest challenges when sorting through the footage, as many of the shots were of the same individuals or of the same actions, so I decided to tackle this problem through audio and creating interesting compositions in post production.
