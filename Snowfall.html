Snowfall

February 2022

A pair of conceptual covers and matching back advertisements representative of the summer and winter seasons for the magazine 'Trope'.

For the summer edition, I was particularly inspired by fashion magazines and modeling shoots that produce fantastical content that seems otherworldly. I used heavy blacks and geometric silhouettes to emphasize this unnatural sensation. The winter edition still continued my desire to emulate a fashion magazine but this time I wanted to portray it from a more human perspective with elements of nature and security.

A pair of conceptual covers and matching back advertisements representative of the summer and winter seasons for the magazine 'Trope'.
