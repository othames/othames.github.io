Nature Track Flyers

September 2025 - Present

I was asked to create promotional flyers for Nature Track, an organization focused on enabling wheelchair users to experience nature, and their partner Freedom Trax, a wheelchair attachment allowing for all-terrain mobility.

With a goal of increasing visibility through unique designs rather than concise branding, I used the different environments surrounding the organization’s home, Santa Barbara, California heavily in the promotional materials. Hiking trails, ocean inlets, and environmental fairs were all used as inspiration while maintaining an emphasis on the tracks.
