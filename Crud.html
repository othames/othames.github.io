Crud.mov

September 2021

An animated bumper for the brand, ‘Crud’. By creating interactions in Photoshop and then exporting these
into Adobe Animate I created most of the movements that didn’t occur
through keyframing.

This work was submitted to the ‘ARTSCLOUD Digital Art Fair’ and I was able to receive a placement of the top 100 out of 3,000 international entries for the Artist Award allowing my work to be showcased in a South Korean exhibition.
